About Us

Company Ethos

Alliance Therapy proudly stands as the first and only clinic of its kind in Nottinghamshire. We specialise in chronic pain, which is defined as any condition where discomfort has persisted for over three months. Whether it’s IBS, fibromyalgia, a stiff neck, or a stubborn bad back, our commitment lies in understanding the underlying causes of your pain, rather than merely addressing the symptoms.

Our approach includes:

  • Thorough assessment process
  • Hands-on pain relief
  • Stress management
  • Breathwork
  • Exercise prescription
  • Education
  • Somatic exercises

The traditional healthcare model overlooks many individuals suffering from pain, which is why we adopt the Biopsychosocial Model. This alternative framework recognises the significant role that psychological and social factors play in pain modulation. Instead of concentrating solely on biological aspects, we consider all three influences of pain.

We take a person-centred approach by treating each patient as an individual. Charlotte learns from her patients just as much as she educates them. No question is too trivial, and no concern is unimportant; this is a safe space for you to unwind with complete confidence.
