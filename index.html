The Assessment Process

At Alliance Therapy, we take a different approach to Physiotherapy…

Persistent pain demands a comprehensive assessment, which is why we offer a telephone consultation to clarify your therapy goals and symptoms even before you arrive.

This information is carefully analysed ahead of your appointment, allowing us to design a customised in-person assessment that enables us to explore your condition more deeply than most physiotherapists!

A different approach to physiotherapy

Physiotherapy Assessment Process:

  1. 1

    Schedule an appointment via email or phone

  2. 2

    Participate in a telephone consultation (pre-assessment)

  3. 3

    Attend your in-person assessment

Alliance Therapy collaborates with patients to develop a dynamic care package throughout their physiotherapeutic journey. This includes a range of support options—from exercises and breathwork to lifestyle modifications and manual therapy—ensuring a comprehensive approach.

Our mission is to empower you with the knowledge and tools necessary to understand your body, helping you take control of your symptoms and enhance your quality of life.

Why Alliance Therapy?

  • A unique assessment process: provides an in-depth diagnosis.
  • Hands-on pain relief: this is at the core of our treatment strategies.
  • 1-hour appointments as standard: giving the necessary time to slow everything down and get to the bottom of your symptoms.
  • Same Physical Therapist for every session: no more repeating yourself at every appointment!
  • Person-centred care: Physical Therapy that works for you and your lifestyle.
  • Educational approach: empowers you to understand your condition.
  • 2 weeks to pay: no need to pay on the day.
  • Mental Health modalities (where necessary): to reduce inflammation & therefore pain.
  • Relaxed environment: the clinic is a homely space where you can feel at ease.
