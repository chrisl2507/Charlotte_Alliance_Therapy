Charlotte’s Mission

“From my own personal experiences living with a chronic pain condition, I know the failings in our non-inclusive healthcare system first hand. The standard approach to healthcare in the West is falling far behind. Sufferers of pain are often forced to become self-sufficient in their management, and many are realising how important nervous system health/mental health is when treating inflammatory conditions such as chronic pain.

How can mental health have nothing to do with inflammatory pain when inflammation is controlled by the brain? It simply can’t and research confirms this. Compartmentalising the brain and body as being separate (mental health versus physical health) is both inaccurate and misleading.

It is my life’s work and passion to spread this message and help patients and practitioners alike to understand true brain and body unity in order to treat everyone. Symptoms are never more real than when they are “all in your head”.”

Mission statement from founder, director and sole-practitioner — Charlotte, Chartered Physiotherapist & Sports Massage Therapist
